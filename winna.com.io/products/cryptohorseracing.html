As Users place bets on the CryptoHorseRacing Platform, they are rewarded with Winna Tokens (What are Winna Tokens?) regardless of the Race outcome through a process called Mining. The difficulty of Mining increases with Time, and is broken down into 5 Stages.

Progress through the 100 Levels earning your stripes within the Ranking system & join the Final Rank as a Winna. Be rewarded with perks accordingly as new Milestones are reached.
