What is the Winna Token?

The Winna Token is distributed to all people betting on the Winna.com Platform, through a process called mining.

This process increases in difficulty over time. Once rewarded with Winna Tokens, you may begin earning dividends (paid in ETH) every 24hrs simply by freezing your Tokens on the Platform.

The Winna Token has real-life value, enabling the option to continue earning daily from the Platform, or to trade it in the marketplace.

These Tokens exist on the blockchain, which makes them incredibly protected through the science of cryptography.
